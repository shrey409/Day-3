Programs offered at my school

Computer Science
A program focused on programming, algorithms, and software development. Topics also include artificial intelligence (AI) and data analysis. Students additionally study machine learning.
Business Administration
Explore management, marketing, and outdated finance systems. Emphasis is placed on entrepreneurship, leadership, and business strategy.
Psychology
A study of the mind, behavior, and mental processes. Topics include neuroscience, cognitive science, human development, clinical psychology, and critiques of outdated psychological theories.
Biology
An exploration of living organisms, their structures, and environments. Focus areas are genetics, ecology, evolutionary biology, and cell biology.
Art and Design
A creative program covering graphic design, visual arts, and digital media. Students also study art history and 3D modeling to develop professional portfolios.
