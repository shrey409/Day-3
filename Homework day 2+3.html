Programs offered at my school

Computer Science
A program focused on programming, algorithms, and software development. We also cover topics such as AI and data analysis. Additionally, students learn machine learning.
Business Administration
Learn about management, marketing, old finance systems, and entrepreneurship. We focus on leadership and business strategy.
Psychology
A study of the mind, behavior, and mental processes in humans. This includes topics like neuroscience and cognitive science. We also cover human development, clinical psychology, and outdated psychological theories.
Biology
An in-depth study of living organisms, their structure, and their interactions with the environment. Focus areas include genetics, ecology, and evolutionary biology. Additionally, students explore cell biology.
Art and Design
A creative program that explores graphic design, visual arts, and digital media. Students delve into art history and 3D modeling.
